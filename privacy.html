PlanWell.md Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 13, 2025

The Short Version: PlanWell does not collect, transmit, or store any of your data. Everything stays on your device. We don't use analytics, tracking, or third-party services. Your schedule data never leaves your control.

What Data We Collect

None.

PlanWell is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, access, or transmit any personal information or user data.

Where Your Data Lives

All data created and managed by PlanWell remains exclusively on your device in markdown files that you own and control. This includes:

  • Your class schedules and lesson plans
  • Period definitions and school calendar settings
  • Any notes or content you create within the app

Data Storage

PlanWell uses your device's local file system to read and write markdown files. On iOS, the app may cache schedule data locally for widget functionality, but this data never leaves your device.

You choose where your markdown vault is located. PlanWell accesses only the folders you explicitly grant permission to access.

Third-Party Services

None.

PlanWell does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We don't use:

  • Analytics tools (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Crash reporting services
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud sync services
  • Authentication providers

Network Activity

PlanWell operates entirely offline and makes no network requests. The app does not:

  • Send data to our servers (we don't have servers)
  • Phone home for updates
  • Check license status online
  • Download content from the internet

iOS Widget

The PlanWell iOS widget displays your current and upcoming classes on your home screen. This data is read from your local cache and never transmitted anywhere.

Data You Control

Because all PlanWell data is stored as plain markdown files on your device, you have complete control over:

  • Backups: Use iCloud Drive, Dropbox, Git, or any backup solution you prefer
  • Sharing: Share files however you want - email, AirDrop, cloud storage
  • Deletion: Delete the app and all data disappears (or keep the markdown files)
  • Migration: Move your markdown vault to any device or editor

macOS vs iOS vs Web App

This privacy policy applies to all versions of PlanWell:

  • macOS app: Reads/writes markdown files from folders you select
  • iOS app: Accesses markdown files via iOS file picker
  • Web app: Uses File System Access API (Chrome/Edge) - all processing happens in your browser, nothing sent to servers

Children's Privacy

PlanWell does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Teachers may use the app to manage their class schedules, but student information should never be entered into PlanWell files.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices (for example, if we add optional cloud sync in the future), we will update this policy and notify users through the app and website.

The current version will always be available at planwellmd.com/privacy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at:

Email: contact@planwellmd.com
Website: planwellmd.com

Bottom Line: Your data is yours. We can't see it, we don't want it, and we've built PlanWell to ensure it stays that way.
