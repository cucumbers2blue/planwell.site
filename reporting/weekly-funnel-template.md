## Formula contract

- `YouTube Click Rate = YouTube Outbound Clicks / YouTube Views`
- `Store Conversion = App Units (Paid) / App Store Product Page Views`
- `Revenue per 1k Views = (Proceeds (USD) / YouTube Views) * 1000`

## Weekly decision thresholds

- Keep current messaging if Click Rate and Store Conversion are flat or improving week-over-week.
- Revise headline/CTA copy if Click Rate drops for 2 consecutive weeks.
- Rework offer/pricing framing if Store Conversion drops for 2 consecutive weeks.
- Evaluate success after 4-week baseline window against target proxy improvement of 40-60%.
