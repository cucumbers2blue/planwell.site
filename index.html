Highlights for busy teachers

Markdown you can trust

Human-readable files with YAML frontmatter. No proprietary sync, no hidden database, just notes you already know how to maintain.

Period-aware timeline

Built specifically for rotating timetables. Plan by day cycle or block schedule and keep the structure consistent across terms.

Instant context switching

Toggle between PlanWell Mode and Obsidian Mode without migrating data. The app respects whatever workflow you bring.

Privacy by design

No accounts, no analytics scripts, no surprise network calls. Everything stays local unless you put it in iCloud or Dropbox.
